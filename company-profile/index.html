About Us

What is Ceritain Aja?

Ceritain Aja is a counseling application with integrated professional counselors for highschool or undergraduate student and employees throughout Indonesia. Ceritain Aja guarantees the quality of certified counselors before they can provide counseling services, so that the solutions provided can change your life to be more comfortable and prosperous.
